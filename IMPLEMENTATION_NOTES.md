## Award-pass interaction system

- Experience layer: first-visit preloader, scroll progress and contextual cursor states.
- Navigation: direction-aware hide/reveal, animated labels and a clip-path mobile menu.
- Hero: GSAP sequencing, pointer parallax, chart staging and scroll-linked product depth.
- Work: alternating editorial layouts, subtle perspective tilt, pointer spotlights and contextual cursor labels.
- Cards: asymmetric bento rhythm, reactive lighting and full-surface hover states.
- Process: scroll-scrubbed progress line and active-step emphasis.
- Conversion: magnetic action controls, oversized final CTA and redesigned project form.
- Accessibility: custom motion is disabled for reduced-motion users and cursor effects never run on touch/coarse-pointer devices.
